🏠 Home Page
<img width="928" height="449" alt="Home Page" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7a4c97cd-8f48-48af-948f-f9c7d1f37eb2" />

The landing page welcomes visitors to Happy Hearts and introduces the pet store. It provides quick access to the Shop, Species, About, Contact, Login, and Shopping Cart pages through a clean, responsive navigation bar.

Shop Page
<img width="912" height="446" alt="Shop page" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c28aca8f-1239-42d1-8d35-6b5aab45417f" />

The Shop page displays pet products available for purchase, including aquariums, pet accessories, and supplies. Customers can search for products, adjust quantities using the plus and minus controls, and add items directly to their shopping cart.

🐠 Fish Species
<img width="840" height="445" alt="Fish Page" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/64dd647d-8fa3-4e75-b0a8-e741bbb0ef48" />

The Fish page showcases the available fish species with detailed information such as price, lifespan, water temperature, and care difficulty. Users can search through available fish and add them directly to their shopping cart.

🛒 Shopping Cart
<img width="912" height="446" alt="Shop page" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/597c04d6-d258-47d6-bd8e-971ecae910ee" />

The Shopping Cart displays all selected pets and products. Users can increase or decrease quantities, remove items, view automatically updated totals, and proceed to the checkout page.

Login Page
<img width="509" height="443" alt="Login Page" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e80bb9a8-078b-460c-99c2-46c92332dfc9" />

Users can securely log into their accounts using Supabase Authentication. Registered users must verify their email address before signing in, ensuring secure account management.
